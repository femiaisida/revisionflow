Revision Flow — Free AI Revision Tracker for GCSE & A-Level

Revision Flow (also written as RevisionFlow) is a free, AI-powered revision tracker built specifically for UK students taking GCSE, A-Level and BTEC qualifications. Unlike generic AI tools, Revision Flow is built around the exact specifications of AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC, Eduqas and CCEA.

What is Revision Flow?

Revision Flow is a web app (installable as an app on your phone) that helps GCSE and A-Level students organise their revision, track progress, and use AI to study smarter. It is completely free to use.

Key features of Revision Flow

  • AI study plan generator — enter your subjects and exam dates, and Revision Flow generates a personalised revision timetable using Mistral AI
  • Topic confidence tracker — rate every spec topic from 1–5 and see a heatmap of your strongest and weakest areas across AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC, Eduqas and CCEA
  • Past paper grade tracker — log your past paper scores and Revision Flow automatically calculates your grade using real grade boundaries (2019–2025)
  • Emergency Mode — exam in the next few days? Emergency Mode gives you a predicted grade, your weakest topics, a today-only revision plan, and 3 predicted exam questions
  • AI answer marker — write a practice answer and the AI marks it like an examiner with AO1/AO2/AO3 feedback and a model answer
  • Predicted exam questions — board-specific, topic-specific questions in the exact style of your actual exam paper
  • AI flashcard generator — generate revision flashcards for any topic and any exam board in seconds
  • Revision timer — Pomodoro-style timer with ambient sounds and XP rewards for focus sessions
  • Daily quests and XP system — earn XP for every revision activity, complete daily quests, unlock badges, and compete with friends on the leaderboard
  • Themes and cosmetics — unlock new colour themes and profile icons as you earn XP and badges

Which exam boards does Revision Flow support?

Revision Flow supports all major UK exam boards: AQA, Edexcel (Pearson), OCR, WJEC, Eduqas and CCEA. Both GCSE (grades 9–1) and A-Level (grades A*–E) are fully supported, along with BTEC Tech Award (Level 2) and BTEC National (Level 3).

Is Revision Flow free?

Yes. Revision Flow is completely free to use. There are no ads. A premium plan is planned for the future but the core features will always remain free.

How is Revision Flow different from other revision apps?

  1. It uses real grade boundary data from AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC and CCEA (2019–2025) to calculate your actual grade — not an estimate
  2. It loads the exact topic list from your exam board specification so you can track confidence on every single topic that could come up in the exam
  3. It uses AI (Mistral) with your actual data — your weak topics, your past paper scores, your exam dates — to give personalised advice
  4. It has Emergency Mode — a dedicated feature for the last few days before an exam that no other free revision tool offers
